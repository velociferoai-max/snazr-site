FAQ

Is this book legal advice?

No. It’s an educational resource to help you understand the process and communicate more clearly. For advice specific to your case, consult a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction.

Does this replace a lawyer?

It’s designed for people representing themselves or working alongside counsel to stay organized and informed.

Do I need to know legal jargon?

No. Terms are explained in plain English with examples.
